Privacy checklist

Before sharing or exporting data

  1. Use approved reports only.
  2. Keep personal names, phone numbers, and exact identities out of summaries.
  3. Use area-level location details unless a response team needs precise coordinates.
  4. Share brief outputs only with authorised partners.
  5. Record moderation decisions so the evidence trail stays clear.
