Privacy & Security

Protect survivor information, enforce responsible data sharing, and ensure that only authorised users can access sensitive incident information.

Privacy Checklist

Before Sharing Incident Data

  1. Use approved reports only.
  2. Never include names, phone numbers or personal identifiers.
  3. Share only area-level locations unless authorised personnel require precise coordinates.
  4. Export reports only to authorised organisations.
  5. Maintain a complete moderation and audit trail.
